The technical refinement of Artificial Intelligence and the datified condition of the image has led us to inhabit a world where the near instant and the distant event can be anticipated. The prediction of the immediate and near future is a reality. Future[past] is a video that aims to reflect on predictability in relation to technology based on various footage of historical events that have already occurred, which become future ones thanks to processing by GANS Pix2Pix. This image processing makes it possible to predict the next frame of a video based on the previous content.
